About

The motto of Shashakt is to educate youngsters and adults alike about the many laws and provisions that our judiciary system has for animals and how to go about implementing them in the legal manner. It also aims to let the society understand that it is our duty to be compassionate towards animals, make people aware about every species, educate them with the basic techniques to be used in the rescue of animals.

Shashakt aims to make all these happen by having awareness campaigns every week with a new theme for each week. The founding principle on which Shashakt is being initiated is "Why All Animals Matter". The purview of Shashakt does not stop the focus on dogs and cats but all other species of animals.

This initiative believes in changing the perception of people, which is "Somebody Must Do Something" to "I Must Do Something".
Shashakt focuses on three main factors
Animal Welfare
Animal Rights
Species Education

We humans tend to have the thought, "Why don't somebody do something?". Particularly in the cases of animal cruelty, people tend to wash their hands off and expect others to "do something", without "doing anything" themselves.

Finally, Shashakt aims to shake off this practice of saying “Somebody Must do something” and bring in change by implementing the thought of “I,We Must Do Something” in the minds of people. Together we all can bring about a change.
